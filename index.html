BENEFITS OF

offers potential health benefits based on various published studies undertaken with phloem exudate from coconut trees.

GOOD FOR ENERGY

contains mainly carbohydrates, which are the body’s primary source of energy and the brain’s preferred energy source.

GOOD FOR BASIC LIFE FUNCTION

contains the main electrolytic minerals such as potassium, magnesium, sodium, calcium, phosphate and chloride, which are essential for the good functioning of the heart and for basic life functioning, such as maintaining electrical neutrality in cells, generating and conducting action potentials in the nerves and muscles.

GOOD FOR THE GUT

contains inulin, a natural prebiotic fiber that helps in improving digestion and metabolism to ensure the ideal required level of absorption of glucose reducing the possibility of blood sugar spikes and crashes, which keeps the gut happy.

GOOD FOR CELL PROTECTION

contains antioxidants, which provides cells protection against free radicals, hence protection against many scourges of aging, and which may play a positive role in heart disease, cancer and other diseases.

GOOD FOR GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

contains all important B vitamins that play a vital role in maintaining good health and well-being.

GOOD FOR IMMUNE FUNCTIONING

contains vitamin C, iron and zinc which are essential substances to boost the immune system.

GOOD FOR PHYSIOLOGICAL PROCESSES

contains proteins and amino acids that are considered the building blocks of the human body; they contribute to a lot of physiological processes such as recovery, muscle hypertrophy, fat loss, and strength gains.

GOOD FOR SPORTS PERFORMANCE

contains most of the essential macro- and micro-nutrients in a natural well combined variety manner that provides a significant ergogenic effect for improving or boosting both endurance and anaerobic exercise performance.

GOOD FOR THE SKIN

contains glycolic acid and alpha–hydroxy acid that helps regulate the natural oils so that the skin is not dry or too oily; it magnificently provides the skin with a healthy glow.
Following are some of the possible benefits of applying on the skin:

Exfoliation: The natural exfoliation properties of can help to remove dead skin cells and encourage cell turnover.

Moisturizing: The natural glycolic acid in can help to hydrate and moisturize the skin.
